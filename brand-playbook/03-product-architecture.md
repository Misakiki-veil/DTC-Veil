# 03 产品架构 / Product Architecture

目的：把品牌定位转化成产品类目、价格带、bundle 和首发优先级。

## 产品策略 / Product Strategy

用头纱作为入口产品，但不要停留在头纱。长期要做 **完整 bridal finishing look**。

建议产品比例：

- 60% 现代经典款 / Modern classic
- 30% 有记忆点但可日常接受的 statement 款
- 10% 强话题款 / Conversation pieces

## 核心类目 / Core Categories

### 1. 头纱 / Veils

子类目：

- Plain cathedral
- Pearl
- Lace trim
- Mantilla
- Blusher
- Drop veil
- 3D flower
- Glitter / sparkle
- Colored veil
- Scarf veil

首发优先级：

- Plain cathedral
- Pearl cathedral
- Lace trim cathedral
- Mantilla
- Blusher
- Scarf veil

### 2. 发饰 / Hair Accessories

子类目：

- Hair pins
- Combs
- Headbands
- Bows
- Pearl pieces
- Floral pieces

首发优先级：

- Pins
- Bows
- Combs

### 3. 手套和袖套 / Gloves And Sleeves

子类目：

- Lace gloves
- Pearl gloves
- Tulle gloves
- Fingerless gloves
- Detachable sleeves

首发优先级：

- Lace glove
- Pearl glove
- Tulle glove

### 4. 颈部和肩部配饰 / Neck And Shoulder Pieces

子类目：

- Bridal scarf
- Choker
- Cape
- Shoulder bow

首发优先级：

- Scarf
- Choker

### 5. 实用小配件 / Utility And Emergency

子类目：

- Veil weights
- Pin kit
- Fashion tape
- Touch-up kit
- Swatch kit

首发优先级：

- Swatch kit
- Veil weights

## 价格带 / Price Ladder

入门价格：

- Swatch kit: $5-$10
- Veil weights / small accessories: $15-$35
- Hair pins: $35-$75

核心价格：

- Gloves: $55-$120
- Bows / combs / headbands: $60-$150
- Classic veils: $120-$220

高客单：

- Statement veils: $220-$380
- Custom veils: $250-$500+
- Full look bundles: $250-$600

## Bundle 想法

- Ceremony Look: veil + hair pins
- Reception Switch: bow + gloves
- City Hall Bride: short veil + gloves + choker
- Dramatic Aisle: cathedral veil + veil weights + comb
- After-Party Edit: scarf + bow + earrings

## 定制优势 / Custom Product Advantage

利用供应链优势提供：

- Fast custom
- More color options
- More lace options
- Custom length
- Custom pearl density
- Initial/date embroidery
- Rush orders

英文表达：

> Made for your dress, your timeline, and your version of bridal.
