# Product / Merchandising / Sourcing

## Purpose

This department owns what we sell, how much we buy, who makes it, how much it costs, and when we reorder.

## Responsibilities

- SKU architecture
- Launch assortment
- Initial buy plan
- Supplier sourcing
- Sample development
- Cost negotiation
- MOQ negotiation
- Production timeline
- Quality standards
- Reorder rules
- Sell-through review
- Product lifecycle decisions

## Put Files Here

- SKU plans
- Supplier lists
- Factory comparison sheets
- Sample feedback
- Cost sheets
- PO trackers
- Quality checklists
- Initial buy and replenishment plans
- Merchandising rules

## Agent Role

Future agent role: **Product & Sourcing Agent**.

This agent should connect product decisions with cost, margin, supplier capacity, inventory risk, and launch timing.
