# Creative / Content / Editorial

## Purpose

This department owns how the brand looks, feels, and communicates visually to modern brides.

## Responsibilities

- Creative direction
- Campaign concepts
- Product photography
- Editorial photography
- Short-form video concepts
- UGC briefs
- Creator content direction
- Product page visual assets
- Social feed look and feel

## Put Files Here

- Moodboards
- Shot lists
- Campaign briefs
- Creator briefs
- TikTok/Reels scripts
- Product page image plans
- Pinterest content plans
- Visual references

## Agent Role

Future agent role: **Creative Agent**.

This agent should translate brand positioning and product priorities into content briefs, shoot plans, and visual asset requirements.
