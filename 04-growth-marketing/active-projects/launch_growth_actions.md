# Launch Growth / Marketing Actions

Owner: Growth / Marketing

## Purpose

Start demand generation early without overspending before product proof exists.

## Checklist

- [ ] Reserve social handles after brand name is chosen
- [ ] Build per-SKU keyword map
- [ ] Check search volume and competition by SKU
- [ ] Create coming-soon or waitlist flow by SKU
- [ ] Track waitlist signups by SKU
- [ ] Use content saves and signup behavior to inform launch buy split
- [ ] Prepare SEO content before launch
- [ ] Prepare Pinterest and TikTok launch content
- [ ] Build creator seeding list
- [ ] Prepare affiliate target list
- [ ] Prepare small paid search test plan
- [ ] Track channel assumptions for finance

## SEO Clock

The domain should be registered as soon as the brand name is confirmed. SEO authority takes time, so the content foundation should not wait for inventory.

## Early Demand Signals

Track:

- Waitlist signup by SKU
- Email capture conversion
- Pinterest saves
- TikTok saves/comments
- Creator feedback
- Product page click interest
- Search impressions once available

## Growth Gates

- [ ] Gate 1: Domain registered and crawlable
- [ ] Gate 3: Demand signals reviewed before final launch buy split
- [ ] Launch: waitlist announcement and creator seeding ready
